📜 About the mod

SMP Essentials bundles everything that makes survival multiplayer feel great: a real economy with tangible coins, a bank, shop NPCs with chest-based stock, paid jobs, and classic quality-of-life commands like homes, warps, and teleport requests.

🪙

Currency

Copper, silver, and gold as physical items, plus a wallet and a craftable bank block.

⛏️

Jobs

Earn coins automatically through mining and harvesting.

🏪

Shops

Shop NPCs that use a chest as stock, configurable per shop.

🏠

Quality of life

Homes, warps, /spawn, and player-to-player teleport requests.

The Bank Block 🏦

A craftable block (8× iron around 1× gold coin) that lets you manage your wallet without commands.

  • Right-click the block → bank screen with live balance.
  • Deposit all coins → all your coins are moved into your wallet.
  • Withdraw → take out 1 copper/silver/gold per click as a physical coin.

🔨 Auction House done

A player-to-player marketplace that runs entirely through your wallet — no loose coins needed, payment transfers automatically from buyer to seller.

CommandWhat it does
/ah sell <price>Lists the item in your hand for sale (price in copper).
/ahView all listings with clickable [Buy] buttons.
/ah buy <id>Buy a listing — it pays from your wallet and the item goes into your inventory.
/ah mineYour own listings, with [Cancel] buttons.
/ah cancel <id>Withdraw a listing and get your item back.
💸 Click [Buy] in chat to purchase instantly. The seller receives the money in their wallet and is notified if online. Listings persist through restarts.

🏪 The Shopkeeper done

A configurable shop NPC, perfect for a shopping district where every player runs their own store.

Placing

  1. Place a chest — this becomes your shop stock.
  2. Look at the top of the chest and type /shopkeeper spawn. The NPC appears where you are looking.
  3. The shopkeeper always looks at you, is invulnerable, and stays in place.

Managing — admin screen

Shift + right-click (as owner or OP):

Buying & stock

Right-click opens the trading screen; shift-click = buy multiple.

🏡 Land & Claims done

Protect your area from griefing — per chunk, with trust for friends.

CommandWhat it does
/claimClaim the chunk you are standing in (16×16).
/unclaimRelease your claim.
/claimsCount your claimed chunks.
/claim showShow colored chunk borders in the world (toggle).
/trust <player>Give a friend build rights in all your claims.
/untrust <player>Remove those rights again.
🛡️ In a claimed chunk, other players cannot break, place, or open chests/doors. With /claim show you can see green borders around your claims and red around other players’ claims — works with any map mod (including Xaero).

🧭 Quality of Life done

🏠

Homes

Multiple named homes with tab completion. /sethome, /home, /delhome, /homes.

🌐

Warps

Server-wide teleport points (set by OP). /warp, /setwarp, /delwarp, /warps.

🏁

Spawn

Quickly return to the world spawn with /spawn.

🤝

TPA

Teleport requests between players. /tpa, /tpahere, /tpaccept, /tpdeny.

↩️

/back

Jump back to your previous location — or to your death spot if you just died.

⚰️

Death Chest

On death, your items do not scatter but land safely in a chest at your death location.

✨ Homes and warps persist through restarts and work cross-dimension (overworld/nether/end), including facing direction. Type /home + Tab for your saved homes. /back remembers your location before every teleport and prioritizes your most recent death spot.

🎯 Bounties done

Put a price on someone’s head — whoever defeats them in PvP claims the reward.

CommandWhat it does
/bounty set <player> <amount>Places a bounty (paid from your wallet). Multiple players can stack rewards.
/bounty listOverview of all open bounties, sorted by amount.
⚔️ Whoever kills the bounty target in PvP (no suicide, no mob kill) receives the full amount directly in their wallet — with a server-wide announcement.

📜 NPC Quests done

A quest NPC that accepts items in exchange for a reward paid to your wallet.

Placing & setup

  1. Look at a block and type /questgiver spawn — the NPC appears there with its own handmade skin.
  2. Hold the required item: /questgiver setitem <amount>.
  3. Set the reward: /questgiver setreward <amount>.
  4. Optional: /questgiver toggleonce — make the quest available only once per player.

Turning in

Right-click the NPC: if you have enough of the item, it is taken and the reward is deposited into your wallet (with sound + green particles). Not enough? You will see exactly how many you are still missing. Shift+right-click (owner/OP) shows the current settings.

Quest Seeker — a hidden treasure hunter 🗺️

A special event NPC that you hide somewhere randomly in the world, with its own yellow treasure hunter skin.

CommandWhat it does
/questseeker spawn <radius> <reward> OPHides the Seeker randomly within <radius> blocks of world spawn, on the surface.
/questseeker remove OPRemoves the active Seeker.
🏆 No hints — pure discovery. Every player who finds it (right-click) claims the reward once for themselves. The very first finder gets a server-wide announcement; after that, everyone can still claim their own reward.
